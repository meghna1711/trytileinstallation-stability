The Proper Cleaning Process for Travertine Flooring

08/08/2015 0 Comments in Latest News by Nahomi Morales

For travertine flooring to remain looking elegant and beautiful as ever, you want to adequately and properly give it the maintenance and cleaning it needs. If not properly cleansed and looked after, travertine flooring will lose its appeal little by little, just like any other flooring. So today, we will be giving you some tips to help you give your travertine flooring the best cleaning and maintenance that is required for it to never lose its charm.

First Thing Is First

When your travertine tile is getting installed, make sure you ask the designer or the professional to properly seal your flooring. This will save your flooring from a lot of messes in the future.

What Do You Clean Travertine Flooring With?

The best options to maintain a spotless travertine is to either use a dry mop or a vacuum. If you decide to opt for a vacuum, make sure its wheels are smooth, or else your travertine floor will get damaged by the vacuum cleaner. When preferring the mopping techniques, try to use warm water from time to time. We must mention it though, excessive cleaning will begin to fade out the beauty of travertine flooring so donâ€™t overdo it.

Protect It

Always, always, always put a protective layer between your flooring and any moist object. For instance, a hot coffee mug and the moisture it releases can certainly damage your flooring if you do not put a barrier between the two. Another way you can protect a much trafficked area of your residence where travertine is installed is to use doormats or rugs. There are specific rugs and doormats that are fabricated especially for travertine flooring; you might want to look into that.
