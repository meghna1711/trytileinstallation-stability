  • Ceramic Tile

    Ceramic tile is one of the least expensive tile options for your home or office. It can be used in a broad diversity of floors, walls and countertops. Due to its glossy surface at high temperatures, it can be made in almost every color and pattern. At Try Tile Installation, we will make sure your ceramic tiles are very steady in size and width. Contact us now and get a completely free estimate on your project!

  • Mosaic Tile

    Try Tile Installation is the ideal place for custom tile mosaic designs incorporating tough, porcelain tile, stone tile and more. Our projects won’t be repeatable, and will create a beautiful appearance to your floors or walls. Our specialized team of designers and installers will work together in order to come up with a work of art and add value to your residential or commercial property.

  • Marble Tile

    If you want to give your home or office a more natural look, marble tile is the perfect option. It is a softer tile and the smooth finish adds to cleaning ease as long as the tiles are appropriately preserved. Marble is mainly used in bathrooms where usage doesn’t affect the soft tile. If you are ready to start taking advantage of our marble tile installation services, contact us today and schedule a free estimate!

  • Travertine

    Travertine is a natural stone that comes in a collection of finishes to offer whatever image or style you wish. Because of its adaptability and chic look, travertine tile has become tremendously fashionable nowadays. Travertine provides a natural appearance and softness while being long-lasting. If you are looking forward a travertine installation for your kitchen or bathrooms, look no further and benefit from our quality installation services!

  • Porcelain Tile

    Porcelain tile is a type of ceramic tile. It is stronger and water resistant, while being consistent in size and depth. Porcelain tile is broadly used in areas of humidity and high traffic. They are becoming trendier because of the fresh look, new styles and many applications they offer. If you are ready to start your project with our porcelain tile installation services, contact us now and one of our professionals will be glad to provide you a free estimate.
